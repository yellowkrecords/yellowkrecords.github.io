Konig lives in Kingston, Ontario. She has been producing electronic music since 2013. The year after, she graduated from Ryerson University with a film production degree. In 2014, Konig released deepblackhole EP, the first of her now-familiar catchy, glittery electronic pop, followed soon after by The Headless EP. She spends a lot of time biting her nails and pursuing various art projects.  Yellow K will be releasing a cassette of Konig's work soon. Check back for details! For now, check out all of her work on Bandcamp or other social media listed above.
