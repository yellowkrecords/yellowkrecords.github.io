“Dimensions is a brave and personal self-portrait, raw and alive with the humor and honesty that happens when you’ve got no one to talk to but yourself. ”

- L.A. Record

Long Beach's Dustin Lovelis  (formerly of The Fling) put out his solo debut on Porch Party Records in April 2015 with the vinyl release of Dimensions. An album filled with self-honesty and deprecation, Dimensions blasts away with the jangly Brit Pop-styled "Space Between" and "Plastic Flowers" but moves skillfully through wide-open, airy spaces on tracks like "Dimensions" and "Comatose." You might think your hearing the humor and range of Lennon or the blunt force of Francis or the sincerity of Chilton. But you'll slowly realize you're just listening to one damn fine album. Yellow K is proud to release Dimensions on cassette tape, available Summer 2015.
