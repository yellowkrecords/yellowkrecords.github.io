Eskimeaux

November 13, 2015

Eskimeaux's "Two Mountains" released on vinyl, tape, and CD

For the first time since it's independent digital release in 2011, Eskimeaux's Two Mountains is available on vinyl, cassette, and CD. Limited Edition Clear Vinyls sold out during pre-order, but standard black 12" vinyl are still available and include a full-color insert with photos and hand-written lyrics by Gabrielle Smith.

William Alexander

November 6, 2015

"Strangest Things" Released Digitally, to Vinyl

After a long wait, William Alexander's Strangest Things is now available. The limited edition LP will comes in translucent purple vinyl. Under the Radar calls it "a lo-fi pop gem" and PopMatters premiered the video for "I Won't Bite." Bandcamp.com also ran a featured interview with William.  Read it here!

New God

April 2, 2015

New God release new single and "Firework" to Cassette

On July 21st, New God will release their new single S U G A R D R E A M Z on a limited edition red cassingle to celebrate the 1 year anniversary of their acclaimed sophomore release Firework and its release to cassette.  Sugardreamz is available for download. The sale goes live at 5:00 pm only at YellowKRecords.com

William Alexander

May 24, 2015

"With A Girl Like You" Cover Premieres on Culture Collide

In anticipation of his forthcoming full-length Strangest Things, William Alexander will release the Come Find Me EP on July 28, 2015. The first, a classic Troggs cover, premiered on Culture Collide. Check it out and stay tuned for more from William!
