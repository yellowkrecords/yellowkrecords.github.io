William Alexander

September 16, 2015

Strangest Things pre-order & release date set

Yellow K Records is pleased to announce William Alexander's Strangest Things will be released on November 6th, 2015. The limited edition LP will come in 180 gram, translucent purple vinyl. The first single "Giant Fade" comes out later this week and will available when pre-orders begin on Friday, September 18th.

New God

Apr 2, 2015

New God release new single and Firework to Cassette

On July 21st, New God will release their new single S U G A R D R E A M Z on a limited edition red cassingle to celebrate the 1 year anniversary of their acclaimed sophomore release Firework and its release to cassette.  Sugardreamz is available for download. The sale goes live at 5:00 pm only at YellowKRecords.com

William Alexander

May 24, 2015

"With A Girl Like You" Cover Premieres on Culture Collide

In anticipation of his forthcoming full-length Strangest Things, William Alexander will release the Come Find Me EP on July 28, 2015. The first, a classic Troggs cover, premiered on Culture Collide. Check it out and stay tuned for more from William!

Dustin Lovelis

Dustin Lovelis releases debut with Yellow K, Porch Party Records

Los Angeles-based Dustin Lovelis released his debut LP Dimensions on vinyl with Porch Party Records on April 24th to much praise. On May 19, Yellow K records will release Dimensions on cassette tape in conjunction with the digital release. Check out the premiere single "Idiot" above!
