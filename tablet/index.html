Apr 2, 2015

Yellow K Brings Eskimeaux's Two Mountains to vinyl

For the first time since it's independent digital release in 2011, Eskimeaux's Two Mountains will be available on vinyl and cassette. The cult classic will come in standard black 12" vinyl and limited edition clear, including a full-color insert with photos and hand-written lyrics by Gabrielle Smith. Pre-orders begin soon! New God release new single and Firework to Cassette

On July 21st, New God will release their new single S U G A R D R E A M Z on a limited edition red cassingle to celebrate the 1 year anniversary of their acclaimed sophomore release Firework and its release to cassette.  Sugardreamz is available for download.
