Eskimeaux

Apr 2, 2015

Yellow K Brings Eskimeaux's "Two Mountains" to vinyl

For the first time since it's independent digital release in 2011, Eskimeaux's Two Mountains will be available on vinyl and cassette. The record comes in standard black 12" vinyl and limited edition clear, including a full-color insert with photos and hand-written lyrics by Gabrielle Smith. Pre-order begins September 18th.

William Alexander

September 16, 2015

"Strangest Things" pre-order & release date set

Yellow K Records is pleased to announce William Alexander's Strangest Things will be released on November 6th, 2015. The limited edition LP will come in 180 gram, translucent purple vinyl. The first single "Giant Fade" comes out later this week and will available when pre-orders begin on Friday, September 18th.

New God release new single and "Firework" to Cassette

On July 21st, New God will release their new single S U G A R D R E A M Z on a limited edition red cassingle to celebrate the 1 year anniversary of their acclaimed sophomore release Fireswork and its release to cassette.  Sugardreamz is available for download. The sale goes live at 5:00 pm only at YellowKRecords.com
