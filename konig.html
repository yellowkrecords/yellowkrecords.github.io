Konig lives in Kingston, Ontario. She has been producing electronic music since 2013. The year after, she graduated from Ryerson University with a film production degree. In 2014, Konig released deepblackhole EP, the first of her now-familiar catchy, glittery electronic pop, followed soon after by The Headless EP. She spends a lot of time biting her nails and pursuing various art projects.

 

In December 2015,  Yellow K released Konig's Puberty to cassette and digital download.
