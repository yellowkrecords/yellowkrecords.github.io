Toronto based singer-songwriter Luke Kaplowsky (LUKA) will release his forthcoming album Summon Up a Monkey King on Yellow K Records in March 2016. A self-described "complicated romantic" LUKA possesses a rich, mesmerizing tenor  that his collaborator/producer Stephen Prickett has crafted into a sound that is  both intimate and inviting.  Recalling the earnest pop sensibilities of Belle and Sebastian or The Magnetic Fields, LUKA gracefully creates a musical space where his complex stories and characters can be rendered with empathy and humor.  Look for more on LUKA in the coming months.
