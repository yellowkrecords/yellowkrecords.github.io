Japanese Breakfast

March 25, 2016

Psychopomp full-album stream/premiere on Hype Machine

Japanese Breakfast's highly-anticipated Psychopomp will be officially released on April 1, but you can stream the entire album for free today at The Hype Machine. In the past weeks, the record has received glowing praise from The AV Club, Pitchfork, Stereogum, The Fader, and Spin. Pre-orders are still available.

 

Japanese Breakfast

January 29, 2016

Japanese Breakfast debuts "In Heaven" on Pitchfork, Stereogum

In preparation for the release of their debut album Psychopomp, Japanese Breakfast unveiled the first track from that effort, the stunning "In Heaven." Crafted out of grief, the track elicits praise from Stereogum, noting it as "elegant" and "a beacon of hope." Pitchfork calls it "a lush, sampledelic alt-rock throwback dripping with dewy reverb."

 

Jagged Leaves

January 29, 2016

Long awaited retrospective available for pre-order now

Several years ago, Yellow K records sought out Dan Penta to record a retrospective culled from twenty years of songwriting in the NYC antifolk scene alongside peers like Regina Spektor and Adam Green. We are so proud to finally bring you one of the most distinct voices you're likely to hear. Stream the debut single "Low & Wet" now and pre-order the vinyl, shipping February 26th.

Yellow K Welcomes New Artists GobbinJr and Luka

This year has seen incredible growth at Yellow K.
