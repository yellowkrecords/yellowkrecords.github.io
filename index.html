Dustin Lovelis

May 3, 2015

Dustin Lovelis releases debut with Yellow K, Porch Party Records

Los Angeles-based Dustin Lovelis released his debut LP Dimensions on vinyl with Porch Party Records on April 24th to much praise. On May 19, Yellow K records will release Dimensions on cassette tape in conjunction with the digital release. Check out the premiere single "Idiot" above!

New God

Apr 2, 2015

New God release new single to ultra limited 7" vinyl

On April 7th, New God will release their 2-part single The Magic Sound Came Out on an ultra-limited 7" vinyl. These hand-lathed copies are limited to 20 hand-numbered copies. The single will also be available for download. The sale goes live at 12:00 pm only at YellowKRecords.com

William Alexander

March 1, 2015

Yellow K signs William Alexander, posts album teaser

Long Beach, CA Weirdo-Pop Auteur William Alexander has signed to Maryland based indie pop upstart Yellow K Records. With this comes the announcement of his debut full-length for the label Strangest Things slated for release on 6/23/15. Listen the album teaser above.
