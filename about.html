why we do it

Yellow K Records is a label built by brothers. Dedicated to the

memory of a brother lost and ran by brothers found.

Yellow K Records was founded for one simple purpose: to release records we want to hear. Period. Everyone at Yellow K is a long-time musician, listener, and seeker of great new music, so when we hear something we love, we want to put it out.

Having struggled through the doldrums of the late nineties – when it was still major labels or basically nothing – we at Yellow K understand it's a new world for musicians, but it's still not easy to get your record printed and heard without selling either your belongings or integrity. We want to change that. A label by artists, for artists.
